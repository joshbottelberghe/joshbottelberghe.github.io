About

I'm a software engineer focused on networking and distributed systems. For the last few years I've worked on routing infrastructure at a multi-cloud networking startup — owning a high-scale route-visualization pipeline (live routing state from a fleet of nodes, through durable storage, into a cache-compute layer that serves customer APIs) and working down into the lower layers: BGP/FRR internals, node-level routing services, upgrade validation, and CI/CD across a fleet of services.

My center of gravity is systems that have to stay both fast and correct at scale — hundreds of tenants, tens of thousands of routes each, freshness measured in minutes, and no good excuse for an outage. The work I'm proudest of is usually the kind where the win comes from restructuring so the system does less, rather than doing the same thing faster. Alongside the product work I build tooling that multiplies the team — debugging agents, on-call automation, internal developer infrastructure — and I mentor a more junior engineer.
