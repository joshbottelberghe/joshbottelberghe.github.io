Josh Bottelberghe

Software engineer — networking, distributed systems, and infrastructure at scale.

I build the control- and data-plane systems that keep large networks legible: taking live routing state off a fleet of nodes and turning it into something operators and customers can actually reason about. Day to day that means Go, BGP/FRR internals, and the usual at-scale toolkit — Kubernetes, AWS, Redis, S3.

I'm most interested in the seam where networking meets distributed systems: how you keep a pipeline fast and correct when the cost of doing the obvious thing grows with the size of the world instead of the size of the change.
